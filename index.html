Our Story

Khayr Gifts & More was founded by two sisters with a shared passion for creating meaningful connections through thoughtful gifts and self-care products that align with Islamic values.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, Halal products that bring joy, comfort, and spiritual enrichment to your life and the lives of your loved ones.

Every item in our collection is carefully selected or crafted to ensure it reflects the principles of purity, sisterhood, and heartfelt generosity that are at the core of our brand.
